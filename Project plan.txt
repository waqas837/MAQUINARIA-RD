## **Phase 1: Public Website ( )**
### **Buyer Features**
- Equipment browse/search/filter system
- Detailed equipment listing pages with galleries/specs
- "Contact Seller/Request Info" forms on every listing
- User registration/login for buyers

### **Seller Features**
- Equipment submission form for sellers
- Seller dashboard to track their submissions

### **Core Pages**
- Homepage, About, Contact, Blog
- FAQ, Legal pages (Terms/Privacy)

## **Phase 2: User Accounts & Dashboard System ( )**
### **OPERATOR DASHBOARD (Client's Key Requirement)**
*Every registered user gets this dashboard*
- **My Leads Panel**: Table showing all inquiries from buyers
- **Conversation Hub**: Built-in messaging to reply to leads
- **Lead Status Tracking**: Mark as New/Contacted/Closed
- **Equipment Management**: View/respond to specific vehicle inquiries

### **User Profiles**
- Account settings for all users
- Saved searches/alerts for buyers

## **Phase 3: Admin & Management (Weeks 5-6)**
### **Admin Control Panel**
- Approve/manage equipment listings
- View all platform leads and conversations
- Manage users and operators
- Blog/content management

### **Trust & Inspection System**
- Display inspection reports on listings
- Condition transparency features

## **Final Week: Testing & Launch**
- Connect email notifications
- Test lead flow: Buyer → Operator Dashboard
- Final deployment
